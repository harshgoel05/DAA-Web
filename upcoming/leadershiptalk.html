Leadership Talk 1.0- Dr. Ojas Mahapatra (February 01, 2019)

Directorate of Alumni Affairs presented Leadership series 1.0 held on 1st February 2019 at Kattankulathur Campus. The event gave the students a chance to meet the director of a company in person.

Dr. Ojas Mahapatra CEO of photonic innovations was invited as the chief guest to give the students a talk on Leadership and business strategies. The enthusiasm of the students after the event itself proved the success of the event.

The MBA Session-Mr. Chakshu Ishan Kaplas (CSE 2017, Alumnus)

The MBA Session was for budding entrepreneurs. Mr. Chakshu Ishan Kaplas (CSE 2017, Alumnus) who has done his post graduation from XLRI which is among the top 5 BSchools in India. The Session was about living in an MBA college and how students can enter top MBA colleges in India.

The session witnessed some real-life examples given by Mr. Chakshu. Miss Shagun Mathur, founder of Women Empowerment Club, DSA, 4th-year ECE student who has also cracked the XLRI this year under the guidance of Mr. Chakshu was also invited to share her views and give significant suggestions to the students on whether they should opt for placements or go for further studies.

5 myths of studying abroad by Karan Mehta 8th Jan'19

The Directorate of Alumni Affairs conducts Alumni Talk every semester. Mr. Karan Mehta from Monash University gave substantial insight and in-depth knowledge about 5 myths on Studying Abroad. He shared his journey from SRM Institute of science and technology to one of the top universities of Australia.

Briefed the students about the ways to apply and also be sure that they do it with utmost perfection. The session concluded with a personal interaction of students with the alumni. The motive of the alumni talk is to give students in-depth knowledge about a certain profession or career.
