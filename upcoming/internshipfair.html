Internship Fair March 2018

The second internship fair was conducted with great success in March 2018. More than 300+ students participated in the 2-day event, which took place on the 24th & 25th of March 2018. 10+ companies offered interships to students.

Each company gave a brief presentation about what they do followed by a test and interview. A few companies took online interviews. The companies that participated include
Day 1: Closerlook, also known as Digital Systems Abroad, Onus Solution, Guvi Geek Technologies, PSK Infotech, Pioneer Design
Day 2 : Wittyfeed, Funstay, Motorodi, BCGRC
